Zombie Shooter Apocalypse

Game Description

This is going to be a Zombie Shooter Game using Phaser. This was made to address boredom during COVID-19 through action and adventure in a 2D shooting game.

The Setting

The world has fallen into an apocalypse, Zombies arise from everywhere and you are the last human alive. You need to protect yourself and fight the incoming invaders. This is a zombie shooter game where you have to shoot down the zombies that arise to protect your land. The zombies will look to steal your base for tactical operations. Loosing your base will mean total destruction for human society. A huge wall looms in front of you. Good Luck!!
