Help Page

The Rules

This game is a 2-D zombie shooter game. The zombies will approach you, the main player, from the left of the screen. Your character is positioned on the very right of the screen. The point of the game is to shoot the zombies. Shooting a zombie will award you with 10 points. The movement speed of the zombies increases over time. The goal of the game is to get the highest score that you can possibly get. If a zombie gets to your side of the screen, the game ends.

The Controls

Buttons used: A and D Keys, Spacebar
A and D: These control the movement of your character. Your character only moves horizontally. Character movement is key to positioning your character to aim for the zombies.
Spacebar: This key controls your gun. Pressing the key will fire a bullet from your character in a horizontal direction towards the other end of the screen. If the bullet hits a zombie, the zombie dies and you get points.
